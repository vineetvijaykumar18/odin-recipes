Daddio's Hacked Orange Julius

Description

Orange Julius Recipe perfect for a hot day, and available at a fraction of the cost

Ingredients

  • 6 ice cubes
  • 1 (6 ounce) can orange juice concentrate
  • 1/2 cup milk
  • 1/2 cup table cream
  • 1 banana
  • 1/3 cup white sugar
  • 1 egg
  • 1 tablespoon powdered dairy creamer
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract

Steps

  1. Place ice cubes in a blender; pulse until crushed to snow-like consistency. Add orange juice concentrate, milk, table cream, banana, sugar, egg, powdered creamer, and vanilla extract. Blend on high speed until smooth, about 1 minute.
